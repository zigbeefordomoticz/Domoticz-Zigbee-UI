The aim of the plugin is to bridge a ZigBee coordinator to the Domoticz Home Automation in order to communicate with objects using the ZigBee protocol.

For information around the Zigate Plugin, please refer to Plugin Wiki (in english)

The small icone links to the informations on the Wiki.

How to deal with issues and Questions

  • In case of question and problem please use the Domoticz Forum to ask your questions and/or problem.
